Live On Wednesday

Sitting at the table, consuming a simple glass of wine, one friend looks across the table at another and asks "Want to start a band?" The recipient of the question ponders for a moment, and responds, "Yes." A third, non-musically inclined friend, asks "what's the name the band?" The immediate response: "We don't need a name until we have a gig!" The musically challenged friend turns to the owner of the wine bar and asks: "You want a band to play next Wednesday?" "Sure" is the response. "Now you need a name."

Live On Wednesday was born. A small group of friends who perform when another friend asks them.
